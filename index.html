Simple Waffle Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic waffle recipe combines a light and fluffy batter, cooked to golden perfection in a waffle maker. Optionally, top with your choice of syrup, fresh fruits, whipped cream, or even savory options like fried chicken or cheese.

Preparation time

  • Approximately: 25 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 ½ cups warm milk
  • ⅓ cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional Dressing: Nutella, Caramel, Chocolate Sauce, Poudered Sugar, Maple Syrup

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.: Gather all of the flour, salt, baking powder sugar, eggs, milk, butter and the vanilla for easy access
  2. Dry ingredients: Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar together in a large bowl; set aside.
  3. Preheat: Preheat your waffle iron to desired temperature, make sure to coat in a layer of oil or melted butter
  4. Wet ingredients: Beat eggs in a separate bowl; stir in milk, butter, and vanilla.
  5. Mix them: Pour milk mixture into flour mixture; beat until blended.
  6. Pour: Pour your mixture into your preheated waffle iron.
  7. Cook: Cook waffles until golden and crisp.
  8. Enjoy: Serve immediately and enjoy!
